About

Rob N Wings was established Spring of 2017 delivering quality dishes of chicken and fries for the avid foodie and meat-connoisseur. The birth of RobNWings came about via the attest and praise of plethora of friends and peers whom believed the substantial meals should be shared with public. Rob N Wings has been notorized for the home grown South Side Sauce and Wings in tribute to the reknowned South Side of Brampton which has become a customer-favourite across the vast beautiful GTA along with the array of flavours on the assenting menu.

Services provided

  • Special Events
  • Party Orders
  • Live Cooking

Mission Statement

At Rob N Wings our mission is to tantalize the customers tastebuds with the dedication, hardwork and a touch of love exuded to produce soigne, remarkable and simmering ensemble of chicken wings, tenders and fries. This is done through impeccable service, professionalism and punctuality whether it be during a live-event, catering, or deployment of party orders.
